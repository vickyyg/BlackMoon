About Us

 
Black Moon Cosmetics has been striving since early 2015 to bring you quality Cruelty-Free & Vegan beauty products from the darker side to grace that beautiful face of yours!  We began our journey with our innovative Liquid Lipstick formula and have made our way to Eyes, Body, & Face using only the finest quality ingredients.
 Inspired by the mysteriously magical moon and all its emanating beauty, Black Moon combines the darkly with the colorful & shiny to create an ethereal makeup world you'd have to see in person to believe. Our products deliver an out of this world experience in both presentation and performance. 
We would not be where we are without the ever growing love & support from our moon beauties! Thank you from the bottom of our black little hearts. 🌙
